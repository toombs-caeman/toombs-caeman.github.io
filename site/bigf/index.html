Benjamin Franklin Method

This method for improving writing was described in Benjamin Franklin's Autobiography dissect and reconstruct ----------------------- 1. Take a short piece of writing, whose style you respect, and break it down to bullet points of the main ideas. 2. Set both aside for a few days at least 3. From your notes only, try to re-express the piece as completely as the original 4. Compare your recreation with the original, correcting any faults * note any points missed in your summary * note awkwardness in phrasing, or dullness of vocabulary in the recreation For more complete mastery: * jumble the bullet points and compare and recreate the structure of the essay as well * do not keep notes, but keep the summary in memory only * instead, record your recreation as a speech to force a single chance at recreation * convert the prose to verse instead of bullets to train vocabulary and strength of metaphor and rhythm ---- ## Finished Reconstructions
